CHAYA

Climate Health Adaptation for Workers through AI-Driven Digital Twin Heat Risk Prediction

A multidisciplinary research initiative integrating Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, IoT, Occupational Health, and Climate Science to protect workers from heat stress and occupational hazards.

Explore Project

About CHAYA

CHAYA aims to build an intelligent occupational health surveillance ecosystem using wearable sensors, AI-driven risk prediction, and Digital Twin technology for real-time monitoring and early warning of heat-related illness among workers.

🌡 Climate Change

Assess workplace heat exposure.

🤖 AI & ML

Predict heat illness risk using machine learning.

🛰 Digital Twin

Create a virtual representation of workers and workplaces.

👷 Occupational Health

Support evidence-based preventive interventions.
